A little bit about me

Hi, I'm Ross Nelson, an Interaction Design student and an aspiring UI/UX designer, currently in second year studying at the Ulster University School of Art, Belfast. I would like to be an illustrator and an UI designer one day but, for now, I need to learn the fundamentals and where better than in the Ulster University, being taught by awesome people.
