About Me

Hello, I’m Ross Nelson, an aspiring UI designer and illustrator. I am currently in my second year, studying Interaction Design at the Belfast School of Art.

I am learning new skills in UI and UX design. This is an experience I am thoroughly enjoying because, not only am I developing my knowledge and coding skills, I am also learning about the concept of design.

I like to create simple, beautiful interfaces that have both a high degree of desirablity and usability. I am passionate about illustration, icon design and branding. I enjoy the challenge of taking a project from paper to ultimately creating aesthetically pleasing finished products. I am a highly motivated, enthusiastic student and I am keen to be taught by masters in the industry.
